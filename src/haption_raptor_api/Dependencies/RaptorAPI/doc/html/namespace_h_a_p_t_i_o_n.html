◆ IFSCouplingModalities

enum HAPTION::IFSCouplingModalities : uint16_t
strong

TREX - IFS coupling modality status.

Enumerator
IFS_MANIP_ROBOT 

TREX is coupled in Cartesian Mode and the digital twin in IFS follows the real robot, in Cartesian for the EE and in angular for the joints.

IFS_IPSI_ROBOT 

Monitoring: the real robot follows the trajectory of the digital one; TREX acts only as a gateway.

IFS_MANIP_IPSI_ROBOT 

The digital twin is controlled in Cartesian mode at the EE from the Virtuose and in angular mode from the real robot ; the real robot follows the movements of the digital one ; the Virtuose receives the Cartesian position of the digital twin EE.


IFS_MANIP_IPSI_ROBOT_SIM 

The digital twin is controlled in Cartesian mode at the EE from the Virtuose, but not in articular from the real robot ; the real robot only follows the movements of the digital one ; the Virtuose receives the Cartesian position of the digital twin EE.
